Frozen 2 is Better



I've watched Frozen 2 once a day for 150+ days in a row.

Frozen 2 came out in November 22nd, 2019. I had always loved and enjoyed Frozen
the first movie which came out in November 27, 2013. Never really been a fan of
princesses but man a queen who has ice powers, thats cool. Frozen 2
brought me back to 7yo me singing Let It Go. Now that I'm older and I can actually
do the research, people like Kristen Bell (Anna's Voice) and Idina Menzel (Elsa's
Voice) have made the movie much more special.
